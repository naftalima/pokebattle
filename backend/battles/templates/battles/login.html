The Battle

- Pokemons from Trainer1 will battle against Pokemons from Trainer2

- A battle is a comparison 1 x 1 of the Pokemons in the order defined by the trainers

- It is a 'best of three' where the attack (A1) of PKN1 is compared to the defense (D2) of PKN2, then A2 vs. D1.

In case of draw the PKN with the highest HP wins.
